About Accu Analytical

Accu Analytical was established in 2018, built on the expertise of a highly skilled team specializing in spectroscopy and chromatography. The company is led by its founder and Managing Director, Dr. M. Shekar Reddy, along with his dedicated team in Hyderabad.

Our commitment to quality is reinforced through continuous improvement, employee training, and regular process evaluations, ensuring that every report we deliver reflects precision and integrity

We offer a comprehensive range of analytical testing services including NMR, DIP MASS, LCMS, IR, TGA, DSC, CHNS, HPLC, GC, and Purification Services

At Accu Analytical, we specialize in Structure Elucidation using Mass Spectrometry and Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) Spectroscopy, helping industries achieve deeper molecular insights and accurate compound identification

With advanced technology and a skilled scientific team, we strive to be your trusted partner in analytical excellence
